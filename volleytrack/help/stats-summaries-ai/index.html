After The Match Premium

Stats, Summaries, and AI

VolleyTrack turns the match log into player stats, set context, and coach-friendly summaries.

Quick answer: Finish the match, open the summary, review set scores and player stats, then generate an AI recap when you want coach notes or social copy.

Core stats to review

  • Serving: aces, good serves, and serve errors.
  • Attack: kills, good attacks, attack errors, and hitting context.
  • Defense: digs, blocks, receive ratings, and errors.
  • Flow: set scores, momentum, timeouts, and substitutions.

AI narratives

AI summaries are generated from the match record. They are useful for quick social posts, coach notes, and parent-friendly recaps, but they should be reviewed before sharing.

  1. Open the completed match summary.
  2. Choose the AI analysis option.
  3. Review the generated coach summary and social summary.
  4. Edit externally if you need a different tone or want to remove sensitive context.

Super Fan recaps

Fan-facing recaps are designed for spectators following a player or match story. They are separate from coach analysis and should stay positive, bounded, and appropriate for families.
