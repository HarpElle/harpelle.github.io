Plans And Limits

Premium, Limits, and Ads

VolleyTrack is designed to be useful free, with Premium available for coaches who want higher limits, ad removal, and advanced features.

Quick answer: Free users can score matches and try advanced features with limits. Premium unlocks higher limits and removes ads for the coach experience.

Premium settings

Set the app up the way you coach. Premium adds more when you want it.

Free tier

  • Live scoring and rotation tracking.
  • One season.
  • Limited AI narratives, exports, voice-enabled matches, and Fan recaps.
  • Banner ads may appear in free experiences.

Premium

  • Higher limits for AI summaries, voice input, exports, seasons, and recaps.
  • Ad-free coach experience.
  • Monthly, annual, and lifetime purchase options where available.

Ads in Fan mode

Free spectator viewing may show non-interruptive banner ads during live matches. Ads should not cover the live score, block alerts, or interrupt recap generation.

Store prices can vary. The app should show current store or RevenueCat pricing at purchase time. Website copy should stay aligned with the live in-app offers.
