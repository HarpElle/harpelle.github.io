Features

Everything you need courtside

Built for coaches who want real data, parents who want to follow along, and players who want to improve.

🏐

Live Scoring & Rotation

Track score, server, and rotation with an intuitive interface built for the pace of a real match. Log aces, kills, digs, blocks, and more with a single tap.

🎤

Voice Input New

Go hands-free during matches. Speak your stats and let AI convert your voice into play-by-play entries in real time.

📊

Advanced Analytics

Go beyond the box score with Sideout %, Point Scoring %, Hitting Efficiency, momentum charts, and detailed set-by-set breakdowns.

⚡

AI-Powered Narratives

Instantly generate match recaps ready for social media, or detailed technical reports for film study — all powered by advanced AI analysis.

Live Spectator Mode New

Share a 6-digit code with parents and fans so they can follow the match live from anywhere. They can send cheers and get personalized Super Fan recaps.

📋

Team & Season Management

Manage rosters, track multiple seasons, and organize tournaments. Assign jersey numbers, positions, and libero pairings with ease.
