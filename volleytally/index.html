VolleyTally

A fast, intuitive volleyball scoreboard. Track every point, undo mistakes, browse match history, and root for your team — all with a single tap.

Soon

Features

Everything you need courtside

Simple enough for pickup games. Flexible enough for tournaments. Smart enough to remember every match.

🏐

Multi-Set Scoring

Track scores across as many sets as you need. Visual indicators show completed sets and the current set at a glance.

⚙

Flexible Match Rules

Best of 3 or best of 5. Set your win-by margin, points per set, and optional score cap to match any league's rules.

📜

Match History

Every completed match is saved automatically. Browse past results with full set-by-set breakdowns whenever you want to look back.

↩️

Instant Undo

Accidentally tap the wrong side? One tap undoes the last point — no more worrying about stray touches during a fast rally.

❤️

My Team Mode

Pick the team you're rooting for. Your team gets confetti and a celebration when they win; a consolation message when they don't.

🎨

Team Customization

Name your teams and pick from a palette of colors. Every match feels like home court.

💾

Match Persistence

Close the app mid-match and come right back to it. Your game state is saved automatically so you never lose progress.

📳

Serve Tracking

Always know whose serve it is. The serve indicator follows the scoring rules automatically.

🎉

Victory Celebrations

Win a set or match and celebrate with confetti and a shareable victory card for your team group chat.

How It Works

Three taps to game time

No accounts, no setup wizards, no internet required.

1

Set your teams

Pick colors and name your teams. Or just start scoring with the defaults.

2

Tap to score

Tap the top half of a panel to add a point, the bottom half to subtract. Swipe to enter a score manually.

3

Share the win

When the match ends, share a victory card to your camera roll or group chat.
