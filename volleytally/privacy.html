Privacy Policy for VolleyTally

Last updated: February 16, 2026

HarpElle ("we," "our," "us") operates the VolleyTally mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

Data You Provide

VolleyTally does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information. Any team names, game settings, and match configurations you enter are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Device Information

We generate a random unique device identifier (UUID) that is stored securely on your device using the operating system's keychain. This identifier is used solely for managing in-app purchase status. This ID is not linked to your personal identity.

Advertising Data

For free-tier users, the App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for the purpose of serving and measuring advertisements. This data collection is governed by Google's privacy practices. Premium users who have removed ads are not subject to AdMob data collection.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information described above to:

  • Provide the App's core scoring and match tracking functionality
  • Manage in-app purchase and premium feature status
  • Display relevant advertisements to free-tier users

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

Service Provider Purpose
Google AdMob Google Displaying advertisements to free-tier users
Apple StoreKit Apple Processing in-app purchases on iOS
Expo Expo Inc. App framework and over-the-air updates

Google AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad targeting and measurement. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

All match data, team names, scores, and settings are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage provided by your operating system. No match data is transmitted to our servers or any third party. Your in-app purchase status is stored securely in your device's keychain.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You may delete all app data at any time by:

  • Clearing the app's data from your device's settings menu
  • Uninstalling the App

6. Children's Privacy

VolleyTally is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App does not require any personal information to function. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us at support@harpelle.com and we will promptly address the concern.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Ad Personalization

You can control ad personalization through your device's privacy settings. On iOS, you can limit ad tracking in Settings > Privacy > Tracking. You may also remove all advertisements by purchasing the premium upgrade within the App.

Data Access and Portability

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have direct access to your data at all times. No request to us is necessary to access your information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

support@harpelle.com
