## üè† DIY Photography Solutions (No Professional Needed)

### **Option 1: Clean Setup Photos (Recommended)**
**What to create**: Simple, clean workspace shots using your phone
**Setup instructions**:
- **Location**: Well-lit area near a window (natural light is best)
- **Props needed**:

## üéØ Brand-Specific Visual Guidelines

### **Color Harmony**
When selecting any images, ensure they work with your brand colors:
- **Primary**: Pink (#fd6ba7) and Purple (#7e39aa)
- **Neutrals**: Clean whites, soft grays
- **Accents**: Avoid competing colors like bright blues or oranges

### **Style Consistency**
- **Photography style**: Clean, modern, slightly warm
- **Illustration style**: Minimal, flat design or simple line art
- **Overall mood**: Professional but approachable, innovative but trustworthy

### **Technical Requirements**
- **Resolution**: Minimum 1920x1080 for hero images
- **Format**: WebP for web optimization, PNG for graphics with transparency
- **Compression**: Optimize for web without losing quality
- **Responsive**: Ensure images work at all screen sizes

## üéØ Final Recommendations

### **Minimum Viable Visual Strategy**
If you want to keep it simple and effective:
1. **Use your existing app and web project screenshots** (you already have these)
2. **Select 5-7 strategic Shutterstock images** for backgrounds and supporting visuals
3. **Skip personal photography** and focus on work quality
4. **Use clean, minimal design** to let your portfolio speak for itself

### **Enhanced Visual Strategy**
If you want to add more personality:
1. **Add simple DIY workspace photos** (2-3 clean shots)
2. **Expand Shutterstock selection** to 10-15 strategic images
3. **Include process documentation** (screenshots of your development workflow)
4. **Add community elements** (volleyball events, development community participation)

### **Professional Growth Strategy**
As your business grows:
1. **Invest in professional photography** when revenue supports it
2. **Expand portfolio documentation** with each new project
3. **Add video content** (screen recordings, client testimonials)
4. **Build visual brand consistency** across all marketing materials
